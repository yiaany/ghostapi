# Code of Conduct

GhostAPI follows a simple contributor standard: be respectful, constructive, and focused on making the project better.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different experience levels and viewpoints.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Keep discussions technical, specific, and good-faith.
- Accept maintainers' decisions once a direction is chosen.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussion.
- Threats or intimidation.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or community.

To report a concern, contact the maintainers privately using the security/reporting channel listed in `SECURITY.md` if no dedicated community contact exists yet.
